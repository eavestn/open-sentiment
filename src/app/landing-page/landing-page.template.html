How Our Data Works

We ask normal people - the people you talk to - how they feel about your content.

It's that simple.

We put data in front of thousands of individuals. They rate it. We capture it. We do all the dirty work through an easy-to-use platform that keeps your sentiment analysis tool calibrated to the times.

No longer is your content compared against some outdated set.

How You Use It

You're welcome to visit our at-a-glance dashboard. It's a neat tool that offers real-time insight into interesting trends within our data. However, the real power of Open Sentiment is unlocked through our simple API.

Through our API, you can investigate a paticular piece of content and grab interesting demographic-related details. Or, you can just grab an occaisional, parameterized "dump" - you're call. We're here to get you the information you need.
